ABOUT US

It started by simply sharing sourdough loaves – with family, friends and neighbours just over the fence. Today we’re baking and delivering sourdough and baked goods to our fellow northerners in Cupertino, Sunnyvale & Mountain view. We’re inspired by the time-honoured craft and magic of baking. It takes us to a place of childhood joy. The stuff of Peach Bakery stories –familiar, warm and wonderful. We love working with high-quality ingredients from the earth. We love the craft. We love our community. We also don’t think premium-quality bakery products should cost the earth. That’s why we’re doing everything we can to minimise our environmental impact, from our packaging to our processes. Our philosophy: Everything we make is handmade from scratch in small batches, using the best ingredients we can get our hands on. We never use artificial flavorings or colorings.
